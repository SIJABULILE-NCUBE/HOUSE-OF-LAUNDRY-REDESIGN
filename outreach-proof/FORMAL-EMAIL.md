As a quick reminder, my name is Sijabulile Ncube and I am a software development student. I chose to redesign House of Laundry & Dry Cleaners' website as part of my course, because I genuinely admire the range of services you offer and felt a modern website could really do the business justice.

Here is a summary of what I built, what changed from the current site, and how everything works:

**WHAT'S DIFFERENT FROM THE CURRENT SITE**
- A modern, clean design that works properly on phones, not just desktop computers
- Updated details — no more outdated copyright date or old COVID-19 banner
- All images load properly, using real photos of your branches and staff sourced from your website and Instagram page, with your permission
- Your per-kilogram pricing is now shown clearly and upfront, instead of being hard to find
- Your full range of services (household and commercial) laid out clearly, including the ten industries you serve

**HOW THE WEBSITE WORKS**
- Home page: gives a quick overview with your branch count, hours, and delivery details
- Services page: shows all your household services (laundry, ironing, dry cleaning, takkie wash, dyeing, alterations) with real prices
- Commercial page: shows your industrial and commercial linen services, using real photos from your website and Instagram, plus the industries you serve
- Branches page: all four branches with their addresses — visitors can tap "Call" to phone a branch directly, or "Directions" to open Google Maps straight to that location
- Reviews page: a few sample customer reviews with a "like" button, to show how social proof could work on the site
- Order page: this is a new feature — customers can select the items they need cleaned, see a running total, and then send the whole request straight to you via WhatsApp or email with one tap. No payment happens on the site; it simply puts together a clear, ready-to-send request so you can confirm pricing and collection as you normally would
- There is also a light mode and dark mode toggle, and a floating WhatsApp button on every page for quick contact

**A FEW THINGS TO KEEP IN MIND**
This is a concept built for a student project, not a live, official website. The logo shown is a recreation I made myself, since I did not want to copy your actual logo file without your permission — if you like the design and would ever want to take it further, you could send me the real logo to drop in, and we could also discuss adding a real contact email if sandile@houseoflaundry.co.za isn't the one you'd like customers to use.

I would love to hear your thoughts, whether it's things you like, things you'd change, or anything that doesn't feel quite right. And if you'd ever be interested in taking a version of this live, I'd be glad to discuss what that would involve.

Thank you again for trusting me with this and for your generosity in sharing photos and your time. It has meant a lot to me as a student.
